Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my admin password?

To reset your admin password, go to Settings > Security > Password Reset. You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password.

How can I add new admin users?

Navigate to Users > Add New User. Fill in the required information and assign the admin role from the permissions section.

How do I export data from the system?

Most sections have an export feature. Look for the Export button in the top right corner of tables. You can export data in CSV, Excel, or PDF format.

How can I customize the dashboard?

Dashboard customization is available through Settings > Appearance > Dashboard. You can rearrange widgets and choose which metrics to display.
