Our Story of Preserving Heritage

For over three decades, Ceylon Antiques has been dedicated to preserving and sharing the rich cultural heritage of Sri Lanka through authentic antiques and collectibles.

Our Journey

Founded in 1990 by renowned antique collector Rohan Perera, Ceylon Antiques began as a small gallery in Colombo dedicated to preserving Sri Lanka's rich cultural heritage. What started as a passion project has grown into Sri Lanka's most trusted marketplace for authentic antiques.

Over the years, we've built relationships with collectors, historians, and preservation societies across the island, allowing us to source the most exquisite pieces while ensuring their historical authenticity.

Today, we continue our founder's vision by connecting discerning collectors with pieces that tell the story of Sri Lanka's fascinating history, from ancient kingdoms to colonial eras.

Our Authentication Process

At Ceylon Antiques, we understand that authenticity is paramount when dealing with historical artifacts. That's why we've developed a rigorous multi-step authentication process that every item must pass before being offered to our clients.

Our team of experts includes historians, material scientists, and conservation specialists who examine each piece for historical accuracy, material composition, and preservation needs.

We provide detailed certificates of authenticity with every purchase, ensuring that you can buy with confidence and preserve your investment for generations to come.
