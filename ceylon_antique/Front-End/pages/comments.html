Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take for my comment to appear?
All comments are reviewed by our team before being published to ensure they meet our community guidelines. This process typically takes 24-48 hours.
Can I edit my comment after submitting?
Unfortunately, once a comment is submitted, it cannot be edited. If you need to make changes, please contact our support team with your request.
What should I include in my comment?
We appreciate detailed comments about your experience with our products, service, and overall shopping experience. Specific details about product quality, authenticity, and delivery are especially helpful for other customers.
Why was my comment not approved?
Comments may not be approved if they contain inappropriate language, personal attacks, promotional content, or information that is not relevant to the product or service. Our team follows strict moderation guidelines.
